Privacy Policy

How we handle your data

Last updated: June 15, 2026

Zero data collection

This app runs entirely inside your device's sandboxed container. No files, photos, camera pixels, PDF pages, or configuration records ever leave your phone. We collect 100% zero data.

No crash tracking or telemetry

Snap Compressor does not integrate any external tracking APIs or telemetry services. Any operational errors or exceptions remain local to your Android system crash log — nothing is ever sent externally.

Media & storage permissions

Storage access is used solely to select images for local compression under Android Sandbox rules. Image data is held in local memory buffers and cleared entirely once compression terminates.

Children's safety

With zero network connectivity and no user registration, Snap Compressor is inherently COPPA and Google Play Families Policy compliant. Minors can safely use the app with no risk of information exposure.
